In this episode of AI Hub, hosts Larry and Amanda delve into the world of AI regulation, featuring insights from cybersecurity expert Mark Weatherford. Discover why he believes states, not the federal government, are taking the lead with over 400 AI-related bills introduced across various states. We explore the implications of California's innovative legislation, the need for harmonization among state laws, and the dangers posed by unregulated AI, including ethical concerns surrounding deep fakes. Weatherford also champions the potential of synthetic data as a solution for navigating privacy challenges. Join us for this enlightening discussion and learn how regulation can educate the public and foster responsible AI development.
